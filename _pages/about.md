### **2. AVSim: Realistic Simulation Framework for Airborne and Vector-Borne Disease Dynamics**  
*(Submitted to IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics: Systems, 2025)*  
**AVSim** is an agent-based simulation platform that models the spread of infectious diseases such as **COVID-19** and **dengue** under **realistic human mobility patterns**.  
The framework integrates **GPS-derived mobility data**, **spectral clustering**, and **graph-theoretic behavior modeling** to achieve realistic human motion and environmental interaction modeling.

- Models **occupation-based mobility** and social heterogeneity  
- Validated with real-world GPS data from 13 profession classes  
- Simulates **vaccination**, **quarantine**, and **vector-control** strategies  
- Demonstrates how AI and signal modeling principles can enhance **epidemic forecasting**
